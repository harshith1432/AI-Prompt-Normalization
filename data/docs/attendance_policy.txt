Attendance Policy for Campus

1. Students must mark attendance for every lecture via the attendance system.
2. Proxy attendance is strictly prohibited; discovery of proxy may lead to disciplinary action.
3. A shortfall below 75% attendance in a subject may result in withholding of exam eligibility.
4. Grace days may be applied up to a small fixed limit with proper justification.
5. Repeated offenses or forged entries will incur fines and referrals to the disciplinary board.
6. Teachers may approve exceptions for documented medical emergencies.
7. Attendance is consolidated at the end of each semester for final eligibility checks.
8. Hallticket issuance depends on meeting minimum attendance requirements.
9. Students are responsible for checking their consolidated records regularly.
10. Contact the office for disputes; allow 7 working days for reconciliation.
