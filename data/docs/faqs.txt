Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use the 'Forgot password' link on the login page or contact admin to reset.

Q: How can I export attendance reports?
A: Go to the Reports page and choose Export → CSV to download consolidated attendance.

Q: How do I mark leave for a student?
A: Submit a leave request via the portal with supporting documents; teacher approval may be required.

Q: What if the camera missed my entry?
A: Contact the attendance office with time and supporting evidence; records can be adjusted.

Q: How often is attendance consolidated?
A: Attendance is consolidated at the end of each semester.

Q: Who do I contact for system setup?
A: Reach out to the IT team for installation and camera setup instructions.
